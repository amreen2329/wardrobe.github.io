What are fitted wardrobes?

Fitted Wardrobes

Fitted wardrobes are made to measure. This means you can get wardrobes that fit effortlessly into the space in question, giving your room a seamless finish. It also means you can utilise any unused nooks and crannies about your house.

What are the benefits of fitted wardrobes over free standing wardrobes?

Benefits of Fitted Wardrobes

The beauty of built-in wardrobes (or any fitted bedroom furniture, for that matter!) is they can be tailored to suit your space. With freestanding wardrobes, you are often restricted in where you can place them. Their dimensions are fixed and will only allow you to use them in certain corners or under ceilings of a particular height.
